About Distribution

These maps are generated from data collected for the 'New Atlas of Australian Birds', the result of over half a million hours of surveys by more than 7,000 BirdLife Australia volunteers, whom we would like to thank for this fantastic effort.

Monitoring of Australian birds through the Atlas project continues, and plays an important role in tracking the effects of changes on bird populations and habitats.
